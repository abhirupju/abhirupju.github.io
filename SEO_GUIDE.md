### 2. **Manual Submission to Google**
1. **Google Search Console**: 
   - Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
   - Add your property: `https://abhirupju.github.io`
   - Verify ownership via HTML file upload or DNS
   - Submit your sitemap: `https://abhirupju.github.io/sitemap.xml`

2. **Direct URL Submission**:
   - In Search Console, use "URL Inspection" tool
   - Enter your homepage URL and click "Request Indexing"

### 3. **Bing Webmaster Tools**
- Submit to [Bing Webmaster Tools](https://www.bing.com/webmasters) as well
- Bing powers several search engines

### 4. **Build Backlinks**
- Link from your university profile page
- Link from Google Scholar profile  
- Link from LinkedIn profile
- Link from conference/paper author pages
- Add to academic directories

### 5. **Content Optimization**
- Regular updates to research/publications
- Add blog posts if possible
- Keep news section current
- Ensure fast loading times

### 6. **Monitor Performance**
- Check Google Search Console weekly
- Monitor "Coverage" and "Performance" reports
- Fix any crawl errors immediately

### 7. **Social Signals**
- Share your site on academic social media
- LinkedIn posts about your research
- Twitter/X engagement with academic community
